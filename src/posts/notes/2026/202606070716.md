My dad gave me this Fisher Space Pen he got in the 70s with this worn off branding on the side that I wasn’t able to recognise or read. Someone on the @penaddict@mastodon.social Slack correctly identified it as Rauma Repola and I was able to find a listing for one on eBay to see what it originally looked like.
