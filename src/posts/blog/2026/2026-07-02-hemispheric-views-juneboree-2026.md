### Conclusion

Like [WeblogPomo](https://rknight.me/blog/tags/weblogpomo/) and [Inktober](https://rknight.me/blog/tags/inktober/), I enjoy the structure of having a single, small thing to do every day but I also need the accountability otherwise I won't do it. Jason did a great job of picking a selection of challenges that every can do and it made the month interesting to go into the forum and see what people had posted every day.
