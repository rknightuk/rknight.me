Today I was met with error code 73 when trying to login to Disney Plus on my Apple TV. Their [help article](https://help.disneyplus.com/en-GB/article/disneyplus-error-73) says you're either on a VPN or "_Attempting to access Disney+ from a country/region where service is currently unavailable_" which I obviously was not.

Despite lots of articles on the web saying to trying rebooting, reinstalling, and the like, none of that worked (although I did try it anyway) because what had actually happened was Disney had blocked my IP address. I know this because when I got on live chat, explained the situation, they asked for my IP (by sending me to [this ad-riddled website](https://www.ipchicken.com)) then they "refreshed my IP" for me which magically fixed it. Yep, refresh the IP address, thats a thing.

No amount of rebooting was going to fix that. Next time, I'll just go straight to the live chat.
