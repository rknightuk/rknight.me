A couple of weeks ago [Ben](https://bencardy.co.uk) was kind enough to send me [a 3-pack of Mildliner Mix pens](https://rknight.me/notes/202604201342/) — two-colour highlighters made by Zebra. This led me down a path of looking for the second set called "Cool" but the price of shipping meant I would have been paying ~£5 a pen which I wasn't willing to do. 

Then this week, as I am want to do, I went over to [Stationery Pal](https://stationerypal.com) (that website is riddled with ads just a heads up) and they had a big banner on the home page about a _ten_ pack of Mildliner Mix so I ordered them immediately. And if my maths is correct, ten is more than six, which is how many I was aware of until that moment. This was the image they had which I downloaded so I could update the [Mildliner site](https://mildliners.rknight.me).

![A chart showing ten different colour combinations for Mildliner mix pens with their names below each one](https://cdn.rknight.me/site/2026/zebra-mildliner-mix-chart-ten-pack.jpg)

I started adding them to the site and noticed that one of the colours I already had, Red and Gold, isn't going to be in the pack I ordered. I went back to [Zebra's website](https://www.zebrapen.com/products/mildliner-mix?variant=42742951084238) and there's no mention of these additional colours, just the original six. So eleven is the number.

I'm down a rabbit hole now thinking about rebuilding the reference site, working out how many of the [dot and stamp marker versions](https://www.zebrapen.com/products/mildliner-dot-stamp-assorted?variant=43095402741966) there are, and if there's a secret extra one in the brush versions like there is in other ranges. Very normal stuff.
