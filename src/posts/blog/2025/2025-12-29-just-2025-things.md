I really need a better structure for these. I find it so hard every year to remember what to include and in which section. Herein I shall attempt to define some kind of structure.

### General

I mostly just kept it together with the lack of sleep from having two children who refuse to sleep much, or at the same time. I did install a new electric awning at the back of the house so that was something. Not much else to report in this area.

### Writing - Online

This section has the "online" suffix because I also have the analog part of writing to talk about but they are very different things.

This is post 73 of the year; less than last year but a bit over 2023. My most popular _new_ posts this year were [Biting the Bullet](https://rknight.me/blog/biting-the-bullet/) which I assume is because of how good the title is, [How Many Mildliner Colours Are There?](https://rknight.me/blog/how-many-mildliner-colours-are-there/) (a lot of traffic from search engines coming into this one), and [I Am Obsessed With this Tomb Raider Pen](https://rknight.me/blog/i-am-obsessed-with-this-tomb-raider-pen/) which of course I eventually bought. I'm very happy that [this post about the 8BitDo keyboard](https://rknight.me//blog/using-the-8bitdo-keyboard-on-macos/) is my most popular post in general now, no longer will I be plagued by [this stupid post](https://rknight.me/blog/convert-spotify-facebook-to-email-login/).

I started doing [weeknotes](https://rknight.me/blog/tags/weeknote/) numbered by the week of my life it is because I can't commit to weekly and I'm happy with how they've come out. They're a useful outlet to post about interesting things I find, like an internet Womble.

### Writing - Analogue

I started bullet journalling[^1] in January which led me to pens, pencils, paper, notebooks, inks, and everything in between. The actual journalling has been really useful in keeping track of things I need to do, ideas I have, things I might want to do. As of this month, I've finished two notebooks and I'll be starting a new one in January. 

I was on The Pen Addict [in March](https://www.relay.fm/penaddict/657) and again [in October](https://www.relay.fm/penaddict/689) and I hope Brad will invite back again at some point because it was a lot of fun. I said it in another post but the pen community are all bloody lovely and welcoming.

I want to try and improve my lowercase handwriting because right now I exclusively write in capitals. 

As a direct result of journalling I ended up [drawing for St Jude](https://rknight.me/blog/get-okay/), participating in [Inktober](https://rknight.me/blog/inktober-completed-it-mate/), and inventing Jeff, a loveable dinosaur. [The sticker fun pack includes a Jeff sticker just fyi](https://knightshift.bigcartel.com/product/crazy-robbs-discount-fun-pack).

Because I only got started this year, I picked up _a lot_ of pens and other related things. I [reviewed some pens](https://rknight.me/blog/one-line-pen-reviews/), a [pen case](https://rknight.me/blog/lihit-labs-compact-pen-case-review/), and even [some sticky tabs](https://rknight.me/blog/bookaroo-sticky-tabs-review/) but the big one was my [Esterbrook Funky Town](https://rknight.me/notes/202510112037/) and [the hottest pink ink I could find](https://rknight.me/blog/hunting-for-the-hottest-pink-ink/). I love this pen so much.

I have my eye on a few other fountain pens for next year including a [Diplomat Viper](https://www.diplomat-pen.com/en/shop/diplomat/viper/) but I expect it will be a much slower year in terms of purchasing. 

### Travel

We did three holidays this year, the first time we've gone away with either of the kids. The first was to [Fisher's Farm](https://www.fishersfarmpark.co.uk/) in one of their cabins. This was exhausting for us and the kids because it's non-stop every day we were there but everyone had a great time. We then did 5 days at both Haven in Bognor and Butlin's, which is also in Bognor. The year of Bognor.

The one definitive conclusion we came to is that we are not "cook while we're on holiday" people. I never want to cook anywhere that isn't my kitchen, so we're just not going to any more. It'll cost more money, but less stress for me.
